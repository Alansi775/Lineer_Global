Biography of Dr. Esaam Moqbel

Dr. Esaam Mohammed Murad, a physician and consultant in community medicine, and a leader in health and industrial development sectors, possesses over 25 years of experience in health management and various development projects in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia and Turkey.

He began his academic career at King Saud University in Riyadh, graduating as a qualified physician, then continued his professional path within the Saudi Ministry of Health in Jeddah, where he held senior supervisory positions in infectious diseases and public health for over two decades, leaving his mark on community prevention and health training.

After retiring from the government sector, Dr. Esaam moved to Turkey where he founded and currently manages Lineer Global, a multi-activity Turkish company operating in:

  • Food and industrial export (vegetable oils, chemicals, sugar, clothing)
  • Specialized health services (training, rehabilitation, and awareness)
  • Educational and developmental fields (vocational training and human development programs)
  • Employment services and community development projects

Dr. Esaam represents investors and funders in existing industrial projects, most notably the Lineer Kostik caustic soda factory in Aliaga – Izmir, and is currently leading regional expansion plans to open Gulf markets, especially Saudi Arabia.

Dr. Esaam believes that sustainable development starts with people, and that export is not just trade, but a message of quality and trust that crosses borders.
